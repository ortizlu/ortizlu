    About Me

    My name is Luis and I’m an aspiring front-end developer from the Washington DC area. I graduated from George Mason University with a Bachelor of Science in Information Systems and Operations Management. Loved learning about the business-side of technology, but was disappointed with the amount of programming I learned. A few weeks after graduation, I decided to teach myself how to code. Now after several months of eye-reddening training, I decided I'm ready to immerse myself in the immensely-rewarding world of Web Development. I am currently looking for a front-end web developer position in the DC/MD/VA area. When I’m not programming, I love spending time with my wife, eating, reading, and getting a workout at the gym.
