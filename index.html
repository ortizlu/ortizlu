About Me

My name is Luis and I’m a front-end developer from the Washington DC area. I graduated from George Mason University with a Bachelor of Science in Information Systems and Operations Management. I loved learning about the business-side of technology, but was disappointed with the amount of programming I learned. A few weeks after graduation, I made the leap to teach myself how to code. After 5 months of self-teaching, I decided to embark in General Assembly's Web Development Immersive Program. After the program I will be in search of a front-end web developer position in the DC area. When I’m not programming, I love spending time with my wife, eating, reading, and getting a workout at the gym.
